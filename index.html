What I did?

Collection of Artwork

Collect artwork by collaborating with government children's homes and art programs. the task of organizing workshops for children. These themes include environmental pollution and unfair labor practices, among others.

Production and Design:

Create high-quality T-shirts using sustainable materials by partnering with ethical clothing manufacturers and designers. Further emphasizing the message, develop compelling artwork taglines or slogans to go with.

Awareness extends beyond

This endeavor goes beyond mere dissemination of information. Its essence lies in endowing the upcoming cohort with the wisdom and resources necessary to make judicious decisions, advocate for morally upright actions,This mission is not limited to a temporary campaign; rather, it aspires to set up a legacy.

How you can you contribute?

The fashion industry finds itself at a crucial juncture. The detrimental impact on the environment and ethical concerns associated with fast fashion are becoming ever clearer. However, many individuals are still oblivious to the untold narrative hidden in their garments. We passionately believe that the voices of children have an extraordinary potential to revolutionize mindsets and instigate transformation. Their unadulterated ingenuity and pure feelings can slice through the cacophony and question prevailing conventions. Our intention is to ignite introspective thought, foster mindful consumption, and forge a path towards a fashionable future that is both sustainable and enduring.
